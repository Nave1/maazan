# מאזן – Monetization & Go-To-Market Strategy

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Philosophy

- **Generous free tier** – Let users experience AI value before paying
- **Value-based pricing** – Price tied to financial outcomes, not features
- **Family-friendly** – Couple/family pricing should feel fair (not 2x individual)
- **Annual discount** – Encourage annual commitment with 2 months free
- **Israeli market calibration** – Priced for Israeli purchasing power

## Competitive Positioning

### Positioning Statement

> **For Israeli households** who struggle to understand and manage their finances,
> **מאזן** is an **AI-powered financial operating system**
> that **transforms complex data into clear guidance and automated planning**,
> **unlike** generic budgeting apps that require financial expertise and don't understand Israeli finance.

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low conversion rate | Medium | High | A/B test pricing, improve free tier value |
| High churn | Medium | High | Focus on habit formation, proactive AI |
| CAC too high | Medium | Medium | Double down on organic/viral, reduce paid |
| Regulation change | Low | High | Legal counsel on retainer, modular architecture |
| AI costs exceed revenue | Low | Medium | Aggressive caching, model routing, usage limits |
| Security breach | Very Low | Critical | Security-first design, insurance, response plan |
| Competitor launches | Medium | Medium | Speed to market, unique Israeli features, AI moat |
