# מאזן (Ma'azan) – Product Vision Document

## Executive Summary

**מאזן** (Ma'azan – Hebrew for "Balance") is an AI-powered Financial Operating System designed exclusively for Israeli households. It transforms personal finance management from a tedious chore into an intelligent, conversational experience where every user has access to their own AI CFO.

Unlike traditional budgeting apps that require financial literacy, מאזן understands the complexities so users don't have to. It converts raw financial data into plain-language insights, actionable recommendations, and automated planning — all in native Hebrew with full RTL support.

---

## Vision Statement

> "Every Israeli household deserves a personal CFO — one that speaks Hebrew, understands Israeli finance, and works 24/7 to optimize their financial future."

---

## Mission

To democratize financial intelligence for Israeli families by building the most comprehensive, AI-native personal finance platform that:

1. **Eliminates financial confusion** – No jargon, no spreadsheets, no manual calculations
2. **Proactively guides users** – The AI anticipates problems and opportunities before users notice them
3. **Adapts to life stages** – From first paycheck to retirement, the platform evolves with the user
4. **Respects Israeli context** – Built from the ground up for Israeli financial instruments, tax laws, and cultural norms

## Market Opportunity

### Israeli Market Size

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Israeli households | ~2.7 million |
| Avg. household income | ₪22,000/month |
| Smartphone penetration | 88% |
| Digital banking adoption | 76% |
| Personal finance app usage | <15% |

### Market Gap

The Israeli personal finance market is severely underserved:

- **No dominant local player** – Most solutions are American imports poorly adapted for Israel
- **Hebrew-first gap** – Existing tools offer Hebrew as an afterthought
- **Israeli financial complexity** – Pension (pensia), Keren Hishtalmut, Kupat Gemel, Bituach Leumi are not understood by foreign tools
- **Cultural mismatch** – Israeli financial behavior (army service timing, apartment purchase culture, family financial structures) is unique

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)

- **TAM**: ₪1.2B (all Israeli households managing finances digitally)
- **SAM**: ₪480M (tech-savvy households ages 25-55)
- **SOM**: ₪48M (Year 3 target – 10% of SAM)

## Competitive Analysis

### Current Landscape

| Competitor | Weakness in Israeli Market |
|-----------|---------------------------|
| Bank apps (Leumi, Hapoalim, etc.) | Limited to single bank, no AI, no planning |
| Caspion | Open banking aggregator only, no AI advisory |
| Foreign apps (Mint, YNAB, etc.) | No Hebrew, no Israeli instruments, wrong categories |
| Excel/Google Sheets | Manual, no intelligence, time-consuming |
| Financial advisors | Expensive (₪500+/hour), inaccessible to most |

### Competitive Advantages of מאזן

1. **AI-Native Architecture** – Not bolted on; AI is the core experience
2. **Israel-Specific Intelligence** – Deep understanding of Israeli financial products
3. **Hebrew Conversational AI** – Natural Hebrew dialogue, not translated English
4. **Family-First Design** – Built for couples and families, not just individuals
5. **Life Event Planning** – Tied to Israeli life milestones (army, marriage, apartment)
6. **Proactive Intelligence** – Alerts and recommendations without user prompting
7. **Financial Timeline** – Unique visual representation of financial future

## Unique Differentiators

### 1. AI Family CFO (שר האוצר המשפחתי)
A conversational AI that acts as a personal financial advisor, speaking natural Hebrew and understanding Israeli financial context deeply.

### 2. Financial Timeline (ציר הזמן הפיננסי)
A living, breathing visual timeline of the user's financial future that updates in real-time based on behavior.

### 3. Life Event Planner (מתכנן אירועי חיים)
Specialized planning modules for Israeli life milestones: wedding, apartment purchase, first child, career transitions.

### 4. What-If Simulator (סימולטור תרחישים)
Interactive scenario modeling that shows the financial impact of major decisions before they're made.

### 5. Financial Health Score (ציון בריאות פיננסית)
A proprietary scoring engine that evaluates overall financial wellness with clear, actionable improvement paths.

### 6. Couple Mode (מצב זוגי)
Purpose-built for Israeli couples managing shared and separate finances with transparent splitting and joint goals.

### 7. Israeli Pension Intelligence (מרכז הפנסיה)
The only consumer platform that aggregates and analyzes pension, Keren Hishtalmut, and Kupat Gemel data together.

## Core Design Principles

1. **Hebrew-First** – Every interaction designed in Hebrew, then adapted to English
2. **Zero Financial Literacy Required** – The AI explains everything in plain language
3. **Proactive, Not Reactive** – The system reaches out to users, not the other way around
4. **Family as Unit** – Household finances are the default mental model
5. **Privacy by Design** – Financial data is sacred; security is non-negotiable
6. **Delight in Simplicity** – Complex calculations, simple presentations
7. **Israeli Soul** – Cultural awareness in every interaction and recommendation
